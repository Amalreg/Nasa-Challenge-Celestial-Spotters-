What You're Seeing

The Cupola is a 360-degree observation deck on the ISS, providing astronauts with an unobstructed view of Earth. From here, they can see:

  • 16 sunrises and sunsets every day
  • Weather patterns forming in real-time
  • City lights at night
  • Aurora borealis and aurora australis

How to Play

  • Enter your weight and click "Start Training"
  • Adjust buoyancy with weights and floaties to achieve neutral buoyancy
  • Use arrow keys to move when properly buoyant (buoyancy between -20 and +20)
  • Collect space samples (blue circles) and use tools (yellow squares)
  • Reach the red repair station to complete your mission!
  • Watch your oxygen levels and avoid obstacles!
